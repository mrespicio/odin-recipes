Egg Sandwich

Egg sandwiches make a fast and yummy. It's also easy to prepare a large quantity!


Ingredients

  • English muffin
  • Eggs
  • Cheese of Choice
  • Deli Meat
  • Optional: Bacon or Turkey Bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Crack eggs into bowl and beat
  3. Add seasonings to bowl
  4. Line a shallow pan and pour egg mixture
  5. Line another pan and add bacon if desired
  6. Bake eggs and bacon at 400 for 8-10mins
  7. Toast english muffin and lay out
  8. When eggs and bacon are ready, remove from oven and let them cool
  9. Cut eggs into equal squares, approximately the same size as your english muffins
  10. Put together the sandwiches - place eggs, cheese, ham, and bacon
