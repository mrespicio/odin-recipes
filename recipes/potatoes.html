Breakfast Potatoes

Home

Potatoes are a staple. Here's one quick way how to make them!

Ingredients

  • Yukon gold potatoes

Seasonings

  • Garlic powder or garlic salt
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into small cubes. Peeling skin is optional
  2. Put potatoes into a pot of salted water and bring to a boil
  3. Melt butter with olive oil in pan with medium heat
  4. When potatoes are soft, drain from water and add to pan
  5. Toss potatoes into butter oil mixture and let them sit without touching to ensure that one side will be crispy
  6. Add seasonings
  7. Now it's done! Serve with bacon and eggs.
